# Pizzeria Sales Performance Dashboard

## Objective
To analyze historical sales data for a pizzeria to uncover revenue trends, identify peak operational hours, and evaluate product performance to drive inventory and staffing decisions.

## Tech Stack
*   **Data Preparation:** Raw data extraction and cleaning.
*   **Visualization:** Power BI (Interactive Dashboard).

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

*   **Total Orders:** 21.35K[cite: 4]
*   **Total Pizzas Sold:** 49.57K[cite: 4]
*   **Average Order Value:** $38.31[cite: 4]

## Key Insights
*   **Peak Operational Windows:** Revenue peaks distinctly on Fridays, reaching $136K[cite: 4]. Daily sales show massive spikes during lunch hours (reaching $112K) and dinner hours (reaching $89K)[cite: 4].
*   **Category & Size Preferences:** The "Classic" pizza category is the strongest revenue driver at $220K[cite: 4]. Customers overwhelmingly prefer "Large" pizzas, with 19.0K units sold, followed by Medium at 15.6K[cite: 4].
*   **Top Performing Products:** "The Thai Chicken Pizza" is the highest-grossing item, generating $43.43K in revenue[cite: 4]. "The Barbecue Chicken Pizza" follows closely behind at $42.77K[cite: 4].
